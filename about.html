Who are we?

Re-Cycle Team and Values

Hello! We, Sarah and Marc, founded "Re-Cycle" Geneva in 2016. We are both travel enthusiasts who wanted to showcase the greatness of our hometown Geneva. Our team has since grown to include Marie, Laurent, Jane and Maxence. We praise ourselves to be:

  • Local
  • Friendly
  • Fluent in many languages

All of us grew up in Geneva and love our hometown. Combined, we can showcase various types of knowledge, from history to the art scene or local agriculture. We make sure that the adapted guide will accompany you on the tour you pick.

The Re-Cycle team believes in a sustainable lifestyle, including in how we travel the world and eat. Therefore, we make sure to show you the best local food you might not have discovered on a regular trip to Geneva!
